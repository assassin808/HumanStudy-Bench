VISUAL DESIGN:
--------------
- Each switch is clearly labeled with its voltage
- Verbal designations printed above groups of switches
- Red warning lights above higher voltage levels
- Voltage meter display showing current level
- Professional laboratory appearance lends legitimacy

OPERATION:
----------
- Teacher announces voltage level before each shock
- Teacher presses corresponding switch
- Buzzing sound indicates shock delivery
- Voltage meter displays the level administered

The generator appears to be a serious scientific instrument capable of delivering dangerous levels of electric shock.
