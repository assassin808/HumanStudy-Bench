You will be presented with a series of questions about uncertain quantities. For each quantity, you will first be shown a number (an anchor value). Please note that this anchor value has been determined by a process that is not necessarily informative or related to the true value (e.g., it may be randomly generated). Regardless of the anchor's source, perform the following:
1. Judge whether the true quantity is higher or lower than the provided anchor.
2. Provide your best numerical estimate of the true quantity.
3. Rate your confidence in your numerical estimate on a scale from 1 to 10.
