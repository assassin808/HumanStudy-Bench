ASCH CONFORMITY EXPERIMENT - PARTICIPANT INSTRUCTIONS
=====================================================

Thank you for participating in this study on visual perception.

TASK DESCRIPTION:
-----------------
In this experiment, you will be making judgments about line lengths. You will see a series of cards, each showing:

1. A STANDARD LINE on the left side
2. THREE COMPARISON LINES (labeled A, B, C) on the right side

Your task is simple: Identify which of the three comparison lines matches the standard line in length.

PROCEDURE:
----------
- You will be seated with several other participants
- The experimenter will show each card to the group
- Participants will announce their judgments aloud in order, going around the room
- When it's your turn, please state your answer clearly (e.g., "Line A", "Line B", or "Line C")
- There will be approximately 18 trials in total

IMPORTANT NOTES:
---------------
- This is a test of visual perception
- The differences in line lengths are quite obvious
- Please make your judgments as accurately as possible
- There are no trick questions - just judge what you see
- Your responses will be recorded by the experimenter

The experiment will take approximately 15-20 minutes.

If you have any questions, please ask the experimenter now.

When you're ready, we'll begin with a couple of practice trials.
